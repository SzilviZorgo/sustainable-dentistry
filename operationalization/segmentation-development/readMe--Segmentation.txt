Some forms of qualitative and unified analyses may require the data to be segmented systematically. As with codes, segmentation parameters may also need to be developed inductively, subjected to social moderation or other forms of triangulation, and then finalized (standardized) before it can be consistently applied.

For more on segmentation, see e.g.: 

Parsing the Continuum: Manual Segmentation of Monologic Data (Zörgő and Brohinsky, 2022)
https://psyarxiv.com/79zxw

Segmentation and Code Co-occurrence Accumulation: Operationalizing Relational Context with Stanza Windows (Zörgő, 2022)
https://psyarxiv.com/smkqr

Frame Analysis - An essay on the organization of experience (Goffman, 1974)
