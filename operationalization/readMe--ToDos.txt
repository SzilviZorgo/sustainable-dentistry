You may want to create a list of tasks involved with your project and keep up to date on who is responsible with what deadline. 
A template is available here: https://osf.io/42967.

A major part of "to-dos" is naming directories and files to keep everything organized. This is more difficult than it sounds, and may require thinking through the whole project's directory structure and considering the past-present-future use of the folder/file.
An excellent resource for organization and naming conventions can be found here:
https://djnavarro.net/slides-project-structure
And another here:
https://speakerdeck.com/jennybc/how-to-name-files
